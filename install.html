How to install (iOS):

1) Click on the small icon that looks like a box with an arrow going upwards located at the bottom of your screen. It is located near the "go forward" history button

2) Click on "Add to Homescreen"

How to install (Android):

1) Click on the banner that appears at the bottom that says "Add to homescreen"

2) If that dialogue does not open, in Chrome, you can click the three dots at the top and click "Install Yoga Your Way"
